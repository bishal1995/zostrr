About Zostrr attempts to bring up the perspective of advertisements in a different paradigm. We in Zostrr try to bring common men into the business of advertising for corporate, start-ups and individuals. The whole idea is to bring together people to use their property like car, personal vehicles and homes to broadcast the promotion of these corporate, start-ups and individuals whom we may technically call the business people. We call upon business people to collaborate with us for their promotion and we give the common men with valuable assets in prime locations to broadcast their message to wider audience in public.
